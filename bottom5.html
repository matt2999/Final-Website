Bottom 5 in Morgantown(in no order)

Applebees

Apple bees has really gone downhill recently in my opinion. Food is often cold and poor service.

Great Wall

Food is often greasy and under/over cooked.

IHOP

All there food except for pancakes are not really that good and I know people that work there that say there are cockroaches in the salad. Definently not my first option.

Wings Ole'

Used to be one of my go too's years ago, now the service is awful, takes forever to get your food and the workers seemed to be high last time I went there.

Shoney's

I have been there a couple of times since they opened and their food is generaly unseasoned and cold from the buffet and the food you can order isn't much better.
