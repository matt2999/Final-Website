Top 5 in Morgantown(in no order)

Mountain state brewing

One of my favorites, Mountain State has some of my favorite pizza of all time and with a great atmosphere and great pizza and sandwiches its a top spot in Morgantown.

Kegler's

On a WVU game day you will always find them packed but with great bar style food it is one of my top picks even not on game day.

Blackbear Burritos

Blackbear is probably my favorite resturant in Morgantown, with great American style Mexican, they are a classic.

Dirty Bird

Great survice and great chicken, one of the best on High street.

Burgers & Bites

Best burgers in town, also some great wings.
